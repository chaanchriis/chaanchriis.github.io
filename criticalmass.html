Nissan Desktop Site Prototype

3 Month Summer internship with Calgary based design agency Critical Mass. Part of in-house development program, Trailhead.

Title

User Experience Design Intern

Client

Nissan USA

Tasks

Navigation Streamlining, Heuristic Evaluations, Wireframing and Prototyping

Skills

User Experience

Overview

During this internship I worked closely with the Nissan USA UX team working on developing wireframes and prototypes as part of a larger site simplification project. I more specifically focused on the navigation aspect of the website and learned how to leverage tools such as website heatmaps, and results of a tree-testing to make informed design decisions.

Major Lessons

- Working within the constraints of a dev team

- Leveraging tools such as site metrics

- Usage of a design system and established branding

Throughout my internship I got the chance to attend seminars offered by the wide variety of disciplines available at Critical Mass, as a part of the Trailhead program for developing emerging talent. Through Trailhead I was paired with a mentor for the duration of my internship, and contributed to the winning in-house intern project for the Clara Lione Foundation.

I am very proud of my work with Critical Mass however I am unable to display it on my portfolio. If you would like to learn more about it please contact me for a call over Zoom.
